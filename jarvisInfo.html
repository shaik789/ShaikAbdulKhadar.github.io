J.A.R.V.I.S

A Virtual Assistant

Description

We are admirers of the Marvel series and especially Tony Stark's assistant JARVIS. It stands for "Just A Rather Very Intelligent System". We have all dreamt of having one Jarvis for our whole lives. So, why not start by building one elemenetary Jarvis ourselves. Though the real JARVIS is built with AI technologies, here in this python project we will simply code it with some basic knowledge and skills of Python.

Features and Working

Speech Recognition is used by JARVIS which is an effective and natural way of people to interact with applications, complementing or even replacing the use of mice, keyboard, controllers and gesture. A hand-free, yet accurate way of communication with applications, speech lets people to productive and stay informed in a variety of situations where other interfaces will not.
